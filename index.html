About us

We are an infrastructure consulting firm with a very talented pool of retired as well as young but experienced civil and structural engineers and architects. It is promoted by senior engineers, designers, architects with more than 30 years of field experience from PWD, private consulting firms of repute with a right mix of young engineers. We help in creating seamless infrastructure from planning and formulating stage to completion in the best interest of the client. We take care of clients’ all requirements from planning to completion stages like pre-feasibility, feasibility study, DPR, soil testing, material testing, designing, drawing, estimating, statutory clearances, BoQ preparation, pre-bidding and post-bidding work, work scheduling and quality control and quality inspection and billing.
